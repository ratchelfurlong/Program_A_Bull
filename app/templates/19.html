Problem Difficulty: Medium

Description:

The game of Yahtzee involves 5 dices, which are thrown in 13 rounds. A score card contains 13 categories. Each round may be scored in a category of the players choosing, but each category may be scored only once in the game.The 13 categories are scored as follows:

ones- sum of all ones thrown
twos- sum of all twos thrown
threes- sum of all threes thrown
fours- sum of all fours thrown
fives- sum of all fives thrown
sixes- sum of all sixes thrown
chance- sum of all sixes thrown
three of a kind- sum of all dice,provided at least three have same value
four of a kind- sum of all dice,provided at least four have same value
five of a kind - 50 points, privided all five dice have same vaue
short stright- 25 points, provided four of the dice form a sequence (1,2,3,4 or 2,3,4,5 or 3,4,5,6)
long stright - 35 points, privided all dice form a sequence (1,2,3,4,5 or 2,3,4,5,6)
full house- 40 points, provided three of the dice are the same and the other two dice are also the same as each other (2,2,5,5,5)

Each of the last six categories may be scored as 0 if the criteria are not met.The score for the game is the sum of all 13 categories plus a bonus of 35 points if the sum of the first six categories is 63 or greater.

Your task is to compute the best possible score for the sequence a sequenec of rounds.
Input- Each line of input contains five ingeters between 1 and 6 indicating the values of the five dice thrown in each round. There are 13 lines for each game, and there may be any number of games in the input data.
Output- Your output should consist of a single line for each game containing 15 numbers: the score in each category (in the given order), the bonus score (0 or 35), and the total score. If there is more than categorization that yields the same total score, any one will do.

Sample Input:

1 1 1 1 1
6 6 6 6 6
6 6 6 1 1
1 1 1 2 2
1 1 1 2 3
1 2 3 4 5
1 2 3 4 6
6 1 2 6 6
1 4 5 5 5
5 5 5 5 6
4 4 4 5 6
3 1 3 6 3
2 2 2 4 6

Sample Output:

3 6 9 12 15 30 21 20 26 50 25 35 40 35 40 35 327
