Problem 28

Problem Difficulty: Hard

Description:

A little turtle is trying to walk along the perimeter of a pond. Given a grid representing the pond (pond is represented by "O"), generate the perimeter string “4-2-2-2-3-2-2-2-2-5” which represents the numbers of pixels of the external (horizontal and vertical) edges of the pond as the turtle moves clockwise.

The turtle will start at the "." directly above the top-most "O" in the left-most column where there exists an "O", walk clockwise around the pond, and stop at the "." it started at.
You can assume that the grid will always be 10x10. Only find the perimeter of a pond that the turtle is currently at, do not find the perimeter of another pond is they are not connected by an "O" (See Sample Input/Output 2).


Sample Input 1:

..........
..OOO.....
..OOOOOO..
......OO..
.....OOO..
....OOOO..
...OOOOO..
...OOOO...
...O......
..........

Sample Output 1:

4-2-4-7-2-2-4-2-3-5-2-2-2-2-2-1-5-4-2

Sample Input 2:

..........
..OOOO....
..OO......
..........
..........
....OOOO..
....OOOO...
...OOOO...
...OOO....
..........

Sample Output 2:

5-3-3-2-4-4-2
